Frequently Asked Questions

Click any question to see the answer

How much does a website cost?

Our projects range from $2,500 to $8,000+ depending on complexity. Business websites start at $2,500, e-commerce at $3,500, and modernization at $2,000. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your needs.

How long does it take?

Most projects take 4-8 weeks from start to launch. Simple business sites take 4-6 weeks, while e-commerce projects take 6-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during planning.

Do you provide hosting?

We can recommend reliable hosting providers and help with setup, or work with your existing hosting. We ensure your site is deployed to a fast, secure server with SSL certificate included.

Will my site be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All our websites are fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices. With most web traffic coming from phones, mobile optimization is a standard part of every project.

What about ongoing support?

All projects include 1-3 months of post-launch support (depending on package). After that, we offer monthly maintenance plans starting at $200/month for updates, backups, and technical support.

Can I update content myself?

Yes! We can integrate an easy-to-use content management system (CMS) that lets you update text, images, and pages without coding. We provide training and documentation.

Do you write content?

We can help structure and optimize your existing content, but copywriting is not included in standard packages. We work with professional copywriters and can coordinate content creation for an additional fee.

What if I need changes later?

We're here for the long term! Minor updates during your support period are included. Larger changes can be handled on an hourly basis or through a retainer agreement.

Do you do SEO?

Basic SEO setup is included in all projects (meta tags, site structure, speed optimization). For ongoing SEO services and content strategy, we can refer you to trusted SEO specialists.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit cards, and PayPal. Payment is typically split: 50% to start, 25% at design approval, and 25% at launch.

Do I own the website?

Yes! Once final payment is complete, you own all source code, designs, and content. We transfer everything to you and provide all necessary files and credentials.

Can you redesign my existing site?

Absolutely! Our Website Modernization service is designed for exactly that. We can refresh your design, improve performance, and add new features while preserving your existing content.

What platform do you use?

We recommend platforms based on your needs. Options include WordPress, Shopify (for e-commerce), Webflow, or custom development. We'll discuss the best solution for your goals and budget.

Do you offer rush projects?

For urgent needs, we can accelerate timelines with our rush service (20% additional fee). This typically reduces project time by 30-40%, though exact timing depends on scope and availability.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We include multiple revision rounds to ensure you're happy. If you're not satisfied after good-faith collaboration, we'll work with you to make it right or provide a partial refund as appropriate.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes, we're happy to sign non-disclosure agreements before discussing sensitive business information. Confidentiality and professionalism are core values of our practice.
