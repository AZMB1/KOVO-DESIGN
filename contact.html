Quick FAQ

Common questions answered

How much does a website cost?

Projects range from $2,500-$8,000+ depending on complexity and features. We'll provide a detailed quote after understanding your needs.

How long does it take?

Most projects take 4-8 weeks from start to launch. Simple sites take 4-6 weeks, while e-commerce projects take 6-8 weeks.

Do you provide hosting?

We can recommend reliable hosting providers and assist with setup, or work with your existing hosting solution.

Will my site be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All our websites are fully responsive and optimized for perfect display on phones, tablets, and desktops.

What about ongoing support?

All projects include 1-3 months of post-launch support. We also offer monthly maintenance plans starting at $200/month.

Can I update content myself?

Yes! We can integrate a user-friendly CMS that lets you update text, images, and pages without any coding knowledge.
